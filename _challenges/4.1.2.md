Expand the `Coordinate` class until all the tests pass.

This will involve adding the following methods to the `Coordinate` class:

- `invert(self)`
- `__str__(self)`
- `__repr__(self)`.
- `__add__(self, other)`
- `__sub__(self, other)`
- `__mul__(self, other)`
- `__truediv__(self, other)`
